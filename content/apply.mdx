## MISSION STATEMENT

[Liar] creates an open, social environment where players can jump in and enjoy everything cooperative gaming has to offer, especially in the world of Guild Wars 2. We recruit for character and community over hours played, seeking active and friendly people who want to play together. Whether you are stepping into the game for the first time, tackling advanced content, or returning from a break, you'll find a helpful and dedicated guild at [Liar].

Our community is a safe space built on positive coexistence, and is a diverse home where women, LGBTQ+ players, gamers with disabilities, and people of all ethnic and religious backgrounds belong. Our Guild is our Strength. We welcome players from all across the globe, but please ensure your account is on a North American server so we can group up. (Plus, we're a pretty rad group to hang out with!)

## ACTIVITIES

* Weekly Guild Missions
* Character Progression
* Living Story and Current Event Achievements
* Open World Meta Events
* Fractals at all Levels
* Raids
* Dungeons
* WvW
* PvP Dailies
* User created Contests and Events

## VOICE COMMS

Our guild is centered around voice chat. We encourage everyone to come on and chat with each other regularly. Please have Discord installed and make sure it is working!

## After Submitting
Once you submit your form, jump into our Discord server to confirm your application. Then join us in the Applicant Lobby for our weekly orientation on Sundays at 12:30 PM EDT!
