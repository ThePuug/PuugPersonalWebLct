# Guild Missions Overview

Guild Missions are our weekly casual community event designed to bring everyone together regardless of level or expansion access. All content takes place in core game areas, so brand-new players can participate right alongside veterans. When it's time to start, simply type ⁠x⁠ in chat to get added to the squad, and hop into our Discord voice channel to listen in. Bringing a character with good map completion makes traveling between mission spots seamless.

# TYPES OF MISSIONS

We rotate through five primary Player Vs Enemy (PvE) mission types each week: Bounties send small groups across maps to hunt down targets. Races transform players into critters to navigate trap-filled obstacle courses. Puzzles and Challenges place us in private guild instances to solve puzzles or clear wave combat, where staying in Discord and waiting for a revive instead of waypointing is essential. Finally, Treks are quick location hunts where the group gathers at specific world marks before an officer activates them.

# Rewards & Commendations

Participating earns you Guild Commendations, silver, and Favor for the guild bank. Commendations can be traded at guild hall vendors for Ascended accessories, recipes, and unique items. Beyond the loot, Guild Missions are the best way to get familiar with the squad, meet fellow members, and enjoy low-stress group gameplay.
