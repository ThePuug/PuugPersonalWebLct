<Panel header="Social culture" key="social-culture" itemKey="social-culture">

While many guilds focus on just one aspect of the game, we offer something deeper: a genuine home away from home. We enjoy a huge range of content together, but at our core, we are a community.

Every member shapes who we are. Our events and casual groups happen naturally whenever people jump in to play together. Check <span className="lr-tag-channel">#looking-for-group</span> or pop into voice to join the fun. If you're anxious to open up, take all the time you need. We're a space built together by the players who call it home.

</Panel>

<Panel header="Inclusivity" key="inclusivity" itemKey="inclusivity">

Our guild is built on mutual respect. Absolutely no discrimination, abuse, or toxic behavior of any kind will be tolerated. At the same time, we want you to feel comfortable being yourself. We're a community of humans, not robots, and we learn as we go. Keep humor in good taste and bring any questions or concerns straight to an officer.

</Panel>

<Panel header="Ranks" key="ranks" itemKey="ranks">

Ranks are how we keep track of activity. We don't require you to represent the guild, but we love to acknowledge those who make us their home.

**Ally / Hibernating** — Alliance members, and a resting spot for those away. We never kick for inactivity.

**Friend** — An in-game rank for regular allies who join our events without representing the guild.

**Rookie** — Where every new member starts, for 7–10 days.

**Member** — Routinely represent, attend events, and chat. Guild stash access and event priority.

**Veteran** — Long-term actives who go above and beyond. Full bank access.

**Officer** — Recruiting, Discord, disputes, guild-building. Interested? Contact @Lucky.

</Panel>

<Panel header="Raiding" key="raiding" itemKey="raiding">

While we aren't a hardcore raid guild, we have a long history of leading successful runs. Our veteran commanders teach full encounter mechanics alongside the right raid mindset.

New raiders are always welcome as long as you put in the work: bring proper gear, learn your build, and respect the content. We run for ~2 hours and clear as much as we can.

</Panel>
